About

Brite Ideas Indy was started by Larry Poe in 2005. He became a distributor through Brite Ideas and his business took off. We now have almost 80 accounts and are continuing to grow. In 2020 his son-in-law joined in partnership.

We design, install, and store your Christmas lights. We make the lights custom-fit to the house. We have houses where we do completely around the house or just the front. We do our best to meet your needs.
